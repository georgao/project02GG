ABOUT US

Branding Ideas

Brand Name: PHONO

Brief Introduction: PHONO is a online store that sells vinyl records. Customers can place orders online and receive their products by shipping, they can also visit retail stores according to their information online.

Target Audience: Music, or Vintage Music lovers. Vintage records, or Vinyl records collectors. Any gender, any age.

Some features:

  • Categorized products with clear hierarchy and searching functions.
  • Recommending popular records, as well as those rare collections.
  • Some special events and offers would be post by limited time.
  • Wish list for members to collect their favorite records before purchasing.

Claim your domain and create a unique web experience your fans will appreciate. Stream tracks, share videos, build your email list, highlight your social media presence, and sell your music and merch.
